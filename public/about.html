DFS - LAX



DFS, or Daily Fantasy Sports, is a term used to describe fantasy sports websites that operate on a day to day basis

FanDuel and DraftKings are among the most popular at this time
While many sports are available to be played on these sites, Lacrosse was not an option

I made this site to offer lacrosse fans a DFS alternative similar to what they would find on other DFS sites

The concept is simple, pick a team before the game starts. When the game starts, your team is locked.
Sit back and Enjoy the game. Then check back later to see how you did
